RybaFish Charts versioning.
 
0.1, 0.2,...
    Major releases are named and numbered like this.
    Merged to master branch.

0.11, 0.12,...
    Hotfix releases. To replace the major release in case of critical/embarrassing bugs.
    Official release date stays the same.

0.1 beta, 0.2 beta
    Beta builds. May be released, may be not. Will not be published on the site.
    No update in the dialog will be made for such builds.
    Builds may be updated, version stays exactly the same.
    Can be distinguished by the build date.

0.1 beta 1, 0.1 beta 2, etc.
    Internal beta builds have a beta+number.
    To be published internally only, not for the public use.
